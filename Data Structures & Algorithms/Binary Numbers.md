
The places in binary are power of 2: 
- 2⁰=1
- 2¹=2
- 2²=4
- ...

- 32 bits = 2³² = more than 4 billion
- 64 bits = 2⁶⁴ = more than 10 billion billion

In SQL, type `int` is 4 bytes vs `bigint` in 8 bytes.
